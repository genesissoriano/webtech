Sto. Tomas Proper Barangay

Sto. Tomas Proper Barangay is more commonly known as Balacbac. The people who first lived in this community got the word balacbac from "bakbak" which is a Kankana-ey word for frog because they said that there are a lot of frogs in here and they can hear the croaking of frogs during the night time.

Sto. Tomas Proper is a barangay that is located in the South-West of Baguio City. It has 6 puroks namely; Balacbac Central,Lower Balacbac,Interior A, Interior B, Interior C and Teacher's Village.

The barangay hall is located at Balacbac Central. Apugan and Barangay Dontogan are the neighbouring communities of Sto. Tomas Proper Barangay.

It is not to be confused with Mt. Santo Tomas, a mountain located further south in the municipality of Tuba. Sometimes tourists get lost because they are confused with the name of the barangay and the mountain.

Human Rights in the Barangay

In every community, every person's right is important. It is a right that is given when a person is born and must not be taken away from an individual by anybody.

Since the oplan TOKHANG was implemented nationwide, a lot of human rights violations was reported. This reports urged the national government to take counter measures to reduce the said violations.There are programs that is given by the Commission on Human Rights (CHR) to the Department of Interior and Local Government (DILG) to ensure that human rights is spread and preserved at the community level. In coordination with the LIGA ng mga Barangay and local government units, a lot of human rights programs have been established in different barangays.

So far, in this barangay, there are no reported human rights violation. Everyone in this community is treated equally, everyone are free to express what they feel in a peaceful manner and everyone is given the opportunity regardless of age, gender and social standing.

There is a Human Rights Desk in the barangay hall that receives and handles human rights related problems reported, if there is any. The one that handles the Human Rights desk is Kagawad Rosa Badecao.

During the interview, Brgy. Capt. Kiswa stated that, “We have to see that every one here in the barangay is treated well as a person, we have to ensure that there are no human rights violation because every person here in the barangay is important. Without the people here in the community then the barangay will not improve”.

Seminars

In the city of Baguio, there are seminars about human rights that are being attended by the barangay officials. Barangay officials of Sto. Tomas Proper attends some of these seminars which is hosted by the City Government of Baguio or NGOs that promotes human rights. These seminars are not only exclusive for the officials but also for the people of Baguio.

Just last year, there was a seminar held in the multi-purpose hall that was attended by people from the different puroks of Sto.Tomas Proper.
