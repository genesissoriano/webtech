What are Human Rights?

Each and every one of us, no matter who we are or where we are born is entitled to the same basic rights and freedom. We are all equally entitled to our human rights without discrimination. Human rights are not privileges and they cannot be granted or revoked.

There are international laws that are layed down by the United Nations to protect and promote the human rights in different countries.
