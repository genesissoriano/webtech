What is Gender Equality?

Gender equality talks about the freedom of both Men and Women to enjoy their rights as a human, equally and correctly. This include the right to life, job and other opportunities that can be done by both genders. Do you know that back in the past, women in the Philippines can't run on any positions in the government? Yes, it was back then, but when Magna Carta of Women was released, the power to run for position was opened and widely accepted for women with equal opportunity as men has.
